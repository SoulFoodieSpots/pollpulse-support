Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

At PollPulse, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Voting Data: Your responses to poll questions and voting preferences
  • Account Information: Anonymous user identifier created through Firebase Authentication
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including questions viewed and time spent
  • Device Information: Basic device type, operating system version, and app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the PollPulse app experience
  • Display aggregated poll results to all users
  • Prevent duplicate voting on the same question
  • Analyze app performance and user engagement
  • Generate new relevant poll questions based on trending news

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • Unsplash: For question images (no personal data shared)
  • News API: For fetching news stories (no personal data shared)

Poll results are aggregated and anonymous. Individual votes cannot be traced back to specific users.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • User identifiers are anonymized and cannot be linked to personal identity
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the data we have collected
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated voting data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics tracking (contact us for assistance)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the button below.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Voting data may be retained in aggregated, anonymous form for historical analysis. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

PollPulse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located in the United States. By using PollPulse, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside of your country of residence.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
