Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I vote on questions?
Simply tap on your preferred answer option. Your vote will be recorded immediately, and you'll be able to see the results.
‚ùì Can I change my vote after submitting?
No, votes are final once submitted. This ensures the integrity of our polling results and prevents vote manipulation.
‚ùì How often are new questions added?
Fresh questions are automatically added every day at 6:00 AM EST. Our AI system generates questions based on the latest trending news stories.
‚ùì Why can't I see the results?
You must cast your vote before viewing the results. This prevents results from influencing your decision and ensures authentic opinions.
‚ùì Are my votes anonymous?
Yes! Your votes are completely anonymous. We only store aggregated results and cannot link votes back to individual users.
‚ùì What categories of questions are available?
We cover 8 categories: Politics, Sports, Business, World, Health, Entertainment, Technology, and Money. You can filter questions by category or view all questions together.
‚ùì How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, please contact our support team using the button above. We'll process your request within 24 hours.
‚ùì Is PollPulse free to use?
Yes! PollPulse is completely free to download and use. The app is supported by non-intrusive advertisements.
‚ùì Why do some questions have ads?
Ads help us keep PollPulse free for all users and cover the costs of generating fresh content daily. We strive to keep ads non-intrusive to your experience.
‚ùì The app isn't loading properly. What should I do?
Try these steps: 1) Force quit and restart the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Update to the latest version. If problems persist, contact support.
